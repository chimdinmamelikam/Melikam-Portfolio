About Me

Hi, I’m Chimdinma Melikam — a Frontend Developer and WordPress Web Designer who enjoys creating clean, modern, and easy-to-use websites.

I work with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to build responsive and interactive interfaces that look great on any device. I also design and develop websites using WordPress, helping individuals and businesses bring their ideas online with style and functionality.

I love solving problems, improving user experiences, and turning simple concepts into real, working web projects. Every website I build teaches me something new, and that’s one of the reasons I enjoy what I do.

When I’m not designing or coding, I’m usually learning something new, researching tools, or working on fun side projects.
